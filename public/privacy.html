Privacy Policy

Stampy is a personal journaling application. Your privacy is important.

Data Collection

Journal entries and photos are stored locally on your device and synced to your private Google Firebase account if logged in. We do not have access to your personal photos or entries.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. Google may use cookies or unique device identifiers to serve ads based on your interests. You can manage your ad preferences in your Google Account settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the app repository.
